For employees

Harassment in the workplace is far too common. But, it’s hard to talk about -- only a fraction of incidents are ever reported. Canary empowers employees to safely speak up about harassment, so that they can use their feedback to build a safer workplace for all. Here’s how it works.

Document

Our anonymous, judgment free tool allows you to document anything that made you uncomfortable. We use legal best practices to make sure we have all the evidence we need, should you decide to come forward.

Match

We store all reports in a database that your employer cannot access. Even our team can’t read it. Our system scans for matches between repeat perpetrators. If we identify matches from your report, we will let you know.

Report

If you decide you want to go forward with a formal complaint, Canary is here to help. We can pass your time-stamped documentation on to Human Resources, so that they have all the evidence they need to investigate.
